Managing Project Data


Researchers collect and analyze a wide variety of environmental data types, each with unique formatting, preprocessing, and metadata requirements. This diversity often creates a significant barrier to adoption of simple data management solutions. Effective data management is critical to the success of any research project and the National Science Foundation (NSF) now requires funded researchers to implement data management plans. VOEIS fills this need, stadardizing data formatting and providing a breadth of data management features for storage, quality control, visualization, and publication for multiple environmental data types.
